About Me

I’m a data analyst with over a decade of experience uncovering patterns and telling stories through data. My career has taken me across investment banks in New York, London, Sydney, and Munich, where I specialized in data engineering and analysis, primarily using kdb+. Along the way, I’ve honed my skills in SQL and Python and developed numerous dashboards to transform complex datasets into actionable insights.

Now based in the Bay Area, California, I’m eager to embrace new challenges and opportunities. Outside of work, I’m passionate about machine learning and excited to deepen my expertise in this evolving field. While I’ve successfully applied ML techniques in past projects, I look forward to exploring cutting-edge approaches and expanding my skill set.

Originally from Ireland, I have a passion for cycling and have recently taken up running, with the SF Marathon in 2025 as my next big goal. At home, my wife and I stay entertained by our two playful kittens, Tayto and Haku, who keep us on our toes. I’m always eager to learn, explore, and connect through data-driven storytelling.
